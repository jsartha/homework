Doraemon: Nobita's New Dinosaur is a Japanese animated science fiction adventure film. It celebrates 50 years of the Doraemon franchise, alongside Stand by Me Doraemon 2. The screenplay for Doraemon: Nobita's New Dinosaur is written by Genki Kawamura. Wikipedia
Release date: 7 August 2020 (Japan)
Director: Kazuaki Imai
Running time: 1h 50m
Languages: Japanese, English
Distributed by: Toho Co., Ltd.
